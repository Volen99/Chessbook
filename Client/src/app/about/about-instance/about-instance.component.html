Description

I created Chessbook to provide a free social media for chess users.

  • We federate selectively - I regularly review other instances and federate with any which have content I believe will appeal to our audience. Other instances are welcome to follow, but we won't automatically follow back. If you would like us to federate with you, please use the Contact Administrator button at the top of the page and we'll be in touch.

  • We currently impose a quota of 20GB for new users. However, we especially welcome content creators, and are happy to provide an increased quota for your vlogs or other personal channels. Simply use the Contact Administrator link at the top of this page and tell us a little about your channel and we'll look at increasing/removing your limit.
